Racing Philosophy

My approach to racing combines aggressive driving with strategic thinking. Every race is an opportunity to showcase both speed and racecraft while respecting fellow competitors.

I believe in continuous improvement, spending countless hours analyzing data, studying track conditions, and refining my technique to find those precious tenths of a second.

Whether it's a local club race or an league event, I bring the same level of preparation and professionalism to every competition.

Looking ahead, my goal is to continue developing as a driver while pushing my limits on track. Through dedication and hard work, I aim to make my mark in the racing world.

Korean Heritage

Seoul, South Korea

오하늘 레이싱

As a Korean racing driver, I take pride in representing my heritage on the track. Based in Amsterdam, I split my time between local competitions and international events, bringing Korean racing spirit to every circuit I compete on.

My racing style combines the precision and dedication ingrained in Korean culture with an aggressive, yet calculated approach to overtaking and race craft.

Being one of the few Korean drivers in the motorsport, I hope to inspire more young talents from Korea to pursue their racing dreams. Through my performances and achievements, I aim to show that Korean drivers can compete at the highest levels of motorsport.
